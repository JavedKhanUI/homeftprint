FAQ

Footprints@HOME is a hybrid learning program that takes the best of virtual learning and hands-on experiences. You will get one kit a month, along with virtual classes with human interaction thrice a week — with flexible schedules!

Flintoclass provides all-in-one kit(s), delivered monthly, with a thematic curriculum, daily lesson plans in day bags, revision-based materials and all items required for hands-on learning.

Though the classes will be facilitated by Flinto experts, the usage of materials will require the involvement and supervision of an adult at home.

Footprints@HOME is available all across India!

All you need to do is contact us at 7550101010 atleast 24 hrs before the class, and we will help you reschedule it.

Footprints@HOME is designed by pedagogy experts, child development professionals, early learning experts, and game designers at Flinto R&D Centre — India’s first and only research centre for early education. You can be assured that all the materials you receive are tested, child-friendly, age-specific, and of high quality!

We have age-specific kits for 1.5-2.5 year olds, 2.5-3.5 year olds, 3.5-4.5 year olds & 4.5-5.5 year olds.
