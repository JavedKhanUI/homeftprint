FAQ

What is Footprints@HOME?

Footprints@HOME is our path breaking program to ensure ongoing learning for young children when they can't go to any pre-school. 'My Footprints Box' is a monthly subscription box service while 'Online Preschool' is Live Interactive Classes. So that you can choose what you prefer.

What do I get in a kit?

Our Kit contains Age-appropriate books, games, worksheets and toys so that child is productively engaged through out the month.

Is parental involvement required?

While we provide the relevant guidelines, some parental support is required in early period so that child gets acclimatised in the new Online environment.

Where are all the programs available?

Footprints@HOME is available all across India!

How do I reschedule a class?

You cannot reschedule a class.

For what age groups is this available for?

We have age-specific kits for 0.5-1.5 year old, 1.5-2.5 year olds, 2.5-3.5 year olds, 3.5-4.5 year olds & 4.5-5.5 year olds.
